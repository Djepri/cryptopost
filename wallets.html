Online cryptocurrency wallets

The safest way is to store and use bitcoins on your personal computer or laptop. But what if you like having your wallet anywhere, anytime, and you don’t want to carry your laptop with you? The best option would be online wallets, which are available from anywhere in the world where there is internet. It is very convenient when traveling, for small online purchases or donations. Such a spare wallet is also useful for working with untrusted services - for example, casinos, HYIPs, faucets and other places where you do not want to "shine" your main wallet or clog it with many small transactions.
